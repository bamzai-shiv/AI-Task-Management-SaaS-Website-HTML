Our Story

TaskAI was born from a simple observation: people spend more time managing their tasks than actually completing them. In 2022, our founders experienced this frustration firsthand while juggling multiple projects and teams.

We envisioned a world where technology doesn't just organize tasks, but actively helps you prioritize, plan, and execute them intelligently. That's when we decided to build TaskAI - a productivity platform powered by artificial intelligence.

Today, we're proud to serve over 50,000 users worldwide, helping them reclaim their time and focus on what truly matters. But we're just getting started.
