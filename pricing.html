Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can try TaskAI Pro free for 14 days. No credit card required. After your trial ends, you can continue with the free plan or upgrade to Pro.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any charges.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. All payments are processed securely.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with TaskAI, contact us within 30 days for a full refund, no questions asked.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is safe! Even after cancellation, you can access your account for 60 days in read-only mode. You can export all your data anytime before final deletion.

Is there a discount for non-profits or education?

Yes! We offer 50% discount for verified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team to apply for the discount.
